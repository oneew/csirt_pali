About CSIRT PALI

Leading cybersecurity excellence across the Americas through collaboration, innovation, and expertise

CSIRT PALI

Computer Security Incident Response Team - Pan American Leadership Initiative

CSIRT PALI is a premier cybersecurity organization dedicated to enhancing digital security across the Americas. We serve as a collaborative hub for cybersecurity incident response teams, fostering information sharing, providing technical expertise, and building resilient cyber defense capabilities throughout the region. Our commitment extends beyond traditional boundaries, creating a unified front against evolving cyber threats while promoting best practices and innovative security solutions.

Our Mission

To strengthen cybersecurity resilience across the Americas by fostering collaboration among CSIRT teams, sharing critical threat intelligence, and providing comprehensive incident response capabilities. We strive to build a unified defense network that protects digital infrastructure and promotes cyber peace in the region.

Our Vision

To be the leading cybersecurity collaborative network in the Americas, recognized for excellence in incident response, threat intelligence, and capacity building. We envision a digitally secure region where organizations can confidently operate in cyberspace, protected by robust, interconnected defense mechanisms.
