CSIRT PALI

CSIRT PALI is dedicated to enhancing cybersecurity across the Americas. Our mission is to foster collaboration among cybersecurity incident response teams, share vital information, and improve incident response capabilities among member countries. We are committed to building a safer and more resilient digital ecosystem through collaboration, training, and knowledge exchange.
